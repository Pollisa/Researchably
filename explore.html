International Genetically Engineered Machine Competition (iGEM)

iGEM is a year-long international competition of student research in synthetic biology.

Young Scientist Competition Thailand (YSC)

Annual national competition of high school research projects of various science fields; ISEF-affiliated fair.

International Conference for Students in Science and Innovation (ISSI)

Annual conference for students to share science and innovation projects and connect with other students in Thailand.

Regeneron International Science and Engineering Fair (ISEF)

World's largest pre-college science fair, showcases student projects from all over the world. Students compete in teams of 1-4 for scholarships, tuition grants, and grand prizes.

Junior Science Talent Project (JSTP)

National research competition and training course for science students. Selected students qualify for scholarships to the PhD level.

Ramathibodi Pitching Challenge (RPC)

Idea pitching competition sponsored by Ramathibodi Hospital on projects surrounding healthcare innovation and healthcare management. Suitable for students interested in medicine.

AI Builders

Program that trains students in artificial intelligence used to solve real-world problems, sponsored by VISTEC and Central Tech.

Asian Science Camp (ASC)

Annual camp for Asian students to receive awards and learn from plenary lectures, laboratory demonstrations, and panel discussions.

FameLab Thailand

International science communication competition for young scientists to present their research to field experts.

Kibo Robot Programming Challenge

Robotics competition that trains students to program free-flying robots in the International Space Station (ISS).

NSTDA Annual Conference (NAC)

Annual series of cutting-edge science and research seminars, hosted by the NSTDA and available to the public.

National Software Contest (NSC)

Annual competition for computer programming projects by high school and college students, hosted by NECTEC.

National Science and Technology Fair (NST Fair)

Largest annual fair in Thailand, displays science and technology exhibitions from organizations across the nation and world.

Origami Competition

Origami competition for teams of 1-3 members of middle and high school students, organized by MTEC.

Prime Minister's Science Award

Competition for research and innovation projects that benefit society and encourages the science and technology industry.

International Congress on Science and Technology-based Innovation (STT)

Annual international conference for young scientists from various fields to meet and exchange knowledge and experiences.

Thai Science Camp

Annual camp for 150 selected high school students to learn from professors and practice science skills.
